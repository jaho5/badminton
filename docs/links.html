Links

    • http://www.badmintonplanet.com/

    Badmintonplanet is a badminton news article site. It posts articles regarding news about the sport and players. However, it has little insight on the article and events themselves.

     

    • https://www.youtube.com/user/bwf/videos

    The official Badminton World Federation youtube channel. This channel is where official high level professional tournament videos are streamed and uploaded.

     

    • http://www.badmintoncentral.com/forums/index.php

    BadmintonCentral is an online badminton forum. It is a community of badminton enthusiasts and discuss things from professional players to equipment.

     

    • http://www.smashbsd.com/

    Smash! is a badminton club in San Diego. It provides training from GBA, global badminton academy, a training program founded by Tony Gunawan, Olympic champion 2000.

     

    • https://www.youtube.com/user/coachingbadminton

    The youtube channel of coach Lee Jae Bok. He goes into and teaches many concepts in badminton. Despite my critique of his video, the content on his channel is quite applicable and is a good start to learning badminton.
